Common questions

Things people ask before they buy.

How is this different from supermarket vinegar?

Most supermarket vinegar is synthetic acetic acid plus flavouring, manufactured in 72 hours. Ours is slow-fermented from whole fruits or grains over 90–365 days, using live cultures. The slow part is what produces live enzymes, organic acids and intact polyphenols.

When in the day should I drink it?

Most people drink it once a day. Morning on an empty stomach, before meals to prepare digestion, or after an oily meal. Pick whatever fits your routine.

Will it taste too sour?

At 30ml in 200ml of water it tastes closer to a fruit drink than straight vinegar. Apple Vinegar and Pomegranate with Berries are the easiest entry points.

Is it Halal certified?

Yes — JAKIM Halal certified. The facility also meets GMP and HACCP standards, and we hold BioNexus status.

How long does one bottle last?

About 30 days at the daily-tonic dose (30ml / day).
